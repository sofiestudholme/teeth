The Pointy Pearlz Collection

A collaboration with Jason Erasmus

In 1998, Pointy Pearlz were carefully implanted into a human surrogate. Over 26 years, these organic gems grew and evolved, their unique shape cultivated by remarkable emergent processes within the donor. In 2024, artist Mr. Erasmus delicately extracted them, preserving their exceptional form. These rare, one-of-a-kind pearls were then transformed into stunning earrings by HEI DIO, blending nature’s elegance with human craftsmanship to create a truly unforgettable piece of wearable art.
